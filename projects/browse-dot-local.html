Browse .local

An Android app for discovering and browsing services on your local network. It automatically finds devices advertising over mDNS — home servers, IoT devices, self-hosted dashboards — and lets you open their web interfaces directly within the app. You can also pin frequently used services as home screen shortcuts for quick access.

Tech Stack

  • Kotlin
  • Android NSD
  • WebView
  • RecyclerView
  • Material Design 3
