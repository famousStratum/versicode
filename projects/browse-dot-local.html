Browse .local

An Android app for discovering and browsing services on your local network. Built for Android 12, where native .local hostname resolution was inconsistently rolled out across devices.

Key Features

  • Automatic mDNS Discovery: Finds devices advertising over mDNS on your local network — home servers, IoT devices, and self-hosted dashboards.
  • In-App Browsing: Open discovered services' web interfaces directly within the app.
  • Home Screen Shortcuts: Pin frequently used services for quick access.

Tech Stack

  • Kotlin
  • Android NSD
  • WebView
  • RecyclerView
  • Material Design 3
